# Precogly Partners

Organizations and individuals who have demonstrated hands-on experience deploying, customizing, and operationalizing Precogly in real-world environments.

## Why Work with a Partner?

Precogly is powerful out of the box, but enterprises often need help with:

- Integrating Precogly with existing tools (Jira, ServiceNow, CI/CD pipelines)
- Building AI-powered threat modeling assistants on the Precogly API
- Training teams on threat modeling methodology and Precogly workflows
- Self-hosted deployment, SSO configuration, and migration
- Threat model reviews, program design, and compliance mapping
