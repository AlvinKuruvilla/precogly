# Precogly Fellows

Individuals recognized for hands-on experience deploying, customizing, and operationalizing Precogly in real-world environments. The first 10 qualifying individuals will be designated **Precogly Founding Fellows**. Contributors thereafter will be designated **Precogly Fellows**. Inclusion in the **Precogly Fellows** list is entirely voluntary. You may opt out at any time. The goal is to recognize those who are helping to shape this project.

## Requirements

Raise at least 5 non-trivial issues on the Precogly GitHub repository, accepted or acknowledged by a maintainer.

To remain listed, fellows must contribute at least once per year. Inactive fellows will be contacted before removal.

## How to Apply

Submit a GitHub issue referencing your 5 qualifying issues.
